About

I have over a decade of experience in product design, with a focus on growing design teams, building design systems, and crafting consumer-facing products. I am currently a product design manager at Meta where I have worked across Reality Labs ecommerce experiences, design systems, and enterprise tooling.

Prior to my current role, I worked in fintech in Chicago, which included building and growing design teams at Avant and Amount.

I got my start in design at a microfinance NGO in China, where I led human-centered design projects focused on improving financial literacy for women entrepreneurs in rural areas.

I have an engineering background and have built projects with React, React Native, JavaScript, Elixir/Phoenix, and other technologies.

Outside of work, I created Daily Chinese, a mobile app that helps Chinese learners grow their vocabulary. It is available on the App Store and Google Play.
