About

I have over a decade of experience designing and building software, with a focus on growing design teams, developing design systems, and creating consumer-facing products.

I am currently a product design manager at Meta, where I have led projects across ecommerce experiences for Reality Labs devices, UI pattern systems, and enterprise tooling. Prior to Meta, I grew design teams at fintech companies focused on consumer lending and financial services.

I got my start in design at a microfinance NGO in China, where I led human-centered design projects to improve financial literacy for women entrepreneurs in rural areas.

I have experience building digital products with multiple technologies and frameworks, including React, React Native, JavaScript, TypeScript, and Elixir/Phoenix.

In my spare time, I created Daily Chinese, a mobile app that helps Chinese learners grow their vocabulary. It is available on the App Store and Google Play.
